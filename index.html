R°

The calm you earn by passing through heat and cold.

Ro — Danish for calm, stillness, peace. A contrast therapy house built on the guided Nordic sauna ritual and the cold that follows it. The degree mark is no accident: Ro lives in the swing between +80° and +4°.

+80°C +4°C
01 — The Essence

Ro is the stillness on the other side of intensity. We guide people through heat and cold — together, in a physical space — and let the body teach what the mind can't. Less biohack, more ritual. Less performance, more presence. A break from the noise, and a way back into the body.

RITUAL

The practice

The guided Nordic ritual — gus masters, heat, breath, and the plunge — as craft, not gimmick.

COMMUNITY

The likeminded

A room of people choosing the same challenge. Connection earned through shared experience.

CRAFT

The wear

Merino wool contrast-therapy wear — natural fibre against skin, never plastic in the steam.

02 — The Mark

R°  —  a degree of calm

The wordmark replaces the “o” in Ro with a degree symbol. It reads as the name, signals temperature, and gives Ro a single ownable glyph: the °.

R°
Primary mark · set in Fraunces
R°
Glyph · icon, avatar, favicon
Ro
Wordmark · in running text
Experience Ro
Lockup · campaigns & partnerships

Do

  • Let the ° breathe — generous clear space all around the mark.
  • Use “Experience Ro” for partnerships, e.g. Nike, powered by Ro.
  • Tint the ° turquoise as an accent on warm-white grounds.
  • Pair the mark with a real temperature readout where it earns its place.

Don't

  • Don't swap the ° for a full circle or a literal “o”.
  • Don't add a tagline lockup that competes with the mark.
  • Don't stretch, outline, or drop-shadow the wordmark.
  • Don't overuse “Experience” — it's a guest, not the name.

05 — Voice & Tone

Playful, knowledgeable, humble

Ro knows the science and the tradition — and never lectures. We invite, we don't preach. We're warm, a little witty, and we put the guest before the brand.

TRAIT 01

Playful

Light on its feet. A wink in the copy. We take the practice seriously, not ourselves.

TRAIT 02

Knowledgeable

Grounded in the ritual and the physiology. We explain, plainly, without the wellness jargon.

TRAIT 03

Humble

The guest is the hero, the guide is the host. Open to everyone, never exclusive.

06 — Art Direction

Steam, skin, water, low warm light

Photography is intimate and elemental — never clinical, never stock. The duality runs through every frame: warm wood and amber light against cool water and pale skin. Bodies are present and human; this is sensual, grounded, and real.

Cedar / warmth
Misty forest
The plunge
Botanical / moss
Open water
Amber light

Shoot

  • Steam, breath in cold air, condensation, water beading on skin.
  • Texture: wood grain, merino weave, wet stone, frost.
  • Natural low light — sunrise gold, blue hour, sauna glow.
  • Real groups, real faces, the quiet after the plunge.

Avoid

  • Bright, even, clinical studio lighting.
  • Gym / performance-tech energy or hustle imagery.
  • Over-saturated turquoise that reads “spa brochure”.
  • Synthetic swimwear in any sauna context.
07 — In Application

From identity to landing page & feed

How the system shows up where it matters first: the experience-ro.com landing page, and the Instagram account that builds the community ahead of it.

Landing page

experience-ro.com · scroll structure
  1. Hero — the R° mark, “Experience Ro,” and one line: the calm you earn. Warm-white ground, the heat→cold spectrum, a single quiet plunge image.
  2. The practice — what contrast therapy is and the guided gus ritual, in plain, knowledgeable copy.
  3. The three Ro's — Experiences (pop-ups & flagship), the Wear (merino), the Community.
  4. Partnerships — the “powered by Ro” model for brands like Nike.
  5. The wear — merino contrast-therapy line; the wool-not-plastic story.
