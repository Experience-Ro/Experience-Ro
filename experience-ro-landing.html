01 — The Practice

Heat opens you up. Cold brings you back. In between is Ro.

Contrast therapy is old — older than any wellness trend. The Nordic sauna ritual, the gus, is led by a guide who works the heat in waves with steam, scent, and breath. Then the plunge: cold water, a sharp inhale, and a calm that arrives like nothing else.

We're not here to optimize you. We're here to give you somewhere to put down the noise — and a guide who knows exactly how far to take the heat and when to send you to the cold.

04 — The Wear

Wool, not plastic.

Most sauna and plunge wear is made from plastic-derived synthetics — heated, sweated in, and pressed against your skin in the one place you came to feel clean.

Your skin can tell the difference. Ro's merino line is the fix we wanted to exist.

Naturally temperature-regulating, odor-resisting, and biodegradable — merino is the fibre the ritual was always meant to be wrapped in.
